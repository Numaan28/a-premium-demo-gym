Our Services

Every service we offer is purpose-built around outcomes. Whether you're training for performance, weight loss, or your first 5K — we have a path built for you.

Everything You Need Under One Roof

A full-spectrum training facility means you never have to look elsewhere. From your first consultation to your peak performance — we've got it covered.

Personal Training

One-on-one coaching tailored entirely to your goals, schedule, and current fitness level. Your trainer designs, adjusts, and coaches every session — no guesswork, just progress.

  • Initial fitness assessment and goal mapping
  • Custom periodized training program
  • Weekly progress reviews
  • Flexible 30, 45, or 60-minute sessions

Strength Training

Build a foundation of real, functional strength through barbell and free-weight programs rooted in proven powerlifting and Olympic lifting methodology.

  • Squat, deadlift, bench, and overhead press focus
  • Progressive overload programming
  • Technique coaching and form analysis
  • Competition prep available

Cardio Programs

Structured cardiovascular programming — from Zone 2 aerobic base building to VO2 max intervals. We use data and heart rate monitoring to make every cardio session intentional.

  • Heart rate zone training
  • Sprint and interval protocols
  • Running, rowing, and cycling options
  • Aerobic base and endurance programs

Weight Loss Coaching

Sustainable fat loss requires more than cutting calories. Our coaches combine training, nutrition timing, and lifestyle habits into a coordinated plan that produces lasting results — not rebound cycles.

  • Calorie and macro structure guidance
  • Metabolic conditioning sessions
  • Biweekly body composition checks
  • Habit and lifestyle coaching

Nutrition Guidance

Derek Chen, our in-house nutrition specialist, works with members to design eating frameworks that fuel performance, support recovery, and align with real life — not just an idealized meal plan.

  • Macro and calorie target setting
  • Pre and post-workout nutrition timing
  • Supplement guidance (evidence-based)
  • Monthly plan reviews and adjustments

Group Classes

Small-group training (max 8 participants) means you get the energy and accountability of a group setting without losing the coaching quality of one-on-one sessions. Classes run seven days a week.

  • HIIT, Barbell Club, and Yoga daily
  • Max 8 members per class
  • Coach-led with individualized cues
  • Included with Performance & Elite plans

Fitness Consultation

Not sure where to start? Our complimentary 45-minute consultation is a no-pressure conversation with a senior coach who'll assess your starting point, listen to your goals, and map the clearest path forward.

  • Completely free — no commitment required
  • 45-minute one-on-one session
  • Movement screen and fitness assessment
  • Personalized program recommendation

Recovery & Mobility

Training hard is only half the equation. Aisha Williams leads weekly mobility sessions, foam rolling workshops, and structured recovery programming to keep you training consistently — without breaking down.

  • Yoga and deep stretch classes
  • Foam rolling and myofascial release
  • Mobility assessment and corrective work
  • Injury prevention programming

Not Sure Which Service Is Right?

Book a free 45-minute consultation and let one of our senior coaches walk you through exactly which program matches where you're at and where you want to go.
